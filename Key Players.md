This angle looks at the environment around people:
```code
social comparison

unrealistic expectations

the pressure to perform

loneliness in crowded cities

the collapse of community

the rise of digital isolation
```

3. Cultural Factors — The Rules We Don’t Talk About
This angle explores the invisible cultural scripts:
```code
“Don’t show weakness”

“Be strong for your family”

“Men don’t talk about feelings”

“Success means suffering quietly”

“Mental health is shameful”
```
This direction is powerful for immigrant communities, South Asian families, and anyone raised with high expectations.
